About

Artistic Haven stands as a cornerstone of Lahore's cultural landscape, its roots tracing back to its inception in 1991. Nestled in the heart of this bustling city, our gallery has flourished into a revered institution, revered for its unwavering commitment to showcasing the unparalleled talent of Pakistani artists. Since our founding, we have served as a dynamic platform for both established luminaries and emerging visionaries, providing them with a nurturing space to showcase their creativity. Our extensive collection reflects the rich tapestry of Pakistani art, spanning genres from classical to avant-garde, and mediums from painting to sculpture. With each stroke of the brush and every sculpted form, we strive to capture the essence of Pakistan's vibrant culture and diverse heritage. Beyond our walls, Artistic Haven serves as a hub for dialogue, inspiration, and community engagement, hosting a myriad of exhibitions, workshops, and cultural events. As we continue to chart our course into the future, we remain steadfast in our mission to celebrate and elevate the artistic soul of Pakistan, inviting all who enter to embark on a transformative journey of aesthetic discovery and appreciation.
